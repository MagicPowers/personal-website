Peter & Helena

Getting Married on July 31, 2019 — Ballyliffen, EIRE

The Wedding Begins In

The Groom

Peter Stewart

Peter is Irish, bookish, and tall. His hobbies include reading, aikido, and avoiding shops. He would like to point out that Helena is good-looking in a rather obvious way whereas his charms are more nuanced. He would like to thank all his friends and family for their advice on wedding traditions, without which the wedding would have been planned in half the time.

"I love your feet because they have wandered over the earth and through the wind and water until they brought you to me." —Pablo Neruda, Your Feet

&
The Bride

Helena Kolberg

Hailing from Estonia, Helena moved to Scotland for her last year of high school. Her hobbies include reading, surpassing Peter in aikido and dragging him into shops. She spends the rest of her time doing normal Estonian things: running barefoot through forests, singing duets with birds, nursing deer back to health, etc. She is aware of your vegan jokes but can’t hear them from way up here on the moral high ground.

"I think I'd miss you even if we'd never met." —Nick, character from The Wedding Date

When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

The ceremony will be held 13:00hrs on Wednesday 31st July at St Mary’s chapel in Lagg, Inishowen – near Malin Head, one of the northernmost points of Ireland.

Wedding Party

The wedding reception will be held after the ceremony in the beautiful Ballyliffin Lodge, in the village of Ballyliffin, Donegal. Guests will be welcomed for drink reception from 14:30hrs, and are invited to mingle until Peter manages to escape the photographer, which might take a while because Helena knows what he’s like. Then, dinner!
